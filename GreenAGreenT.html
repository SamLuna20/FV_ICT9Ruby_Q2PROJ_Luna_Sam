- - - Green Apple Green Tea - - -

Ingredients Measurement
Green Apple Syrup 30 ml
Sugar Syurp 40 ml
Green Tea 120 ml
Ice 3 large pieces
Procedure:
  1. Mix all ingredients using a shaker.
  2. Pour onto a cup
  3. Serve cold. Enjoy!

Prep Time: 20 minutes
