- - - Halo-halo - - -

Ingredients Measurement
Sweetened Red Beans 1/4 cup
Sweetened White Beans 1/4 cup
Shaved Ice 4 cups
Large Banana (thinly sliced) 1 pc.
Drained Jackfruit canned 1/4 cup
Drained Macapuno strips 1/4 cup
Drained Nata de Coco 1/4 cup
Drained Red Palm Seeds 1/4 cup
Drained Green Palm Seeds 1/4 cup
Evaporated Milk 1 cup
Crunch Berries Cereal 1/2 cup
Ube Ice Cream 2 scoops
Procedure:
  1. Place half the sweetened red and white beans at the bottom of each glass.
  2. Process about half the ice cubes in an ice shaver, blender, or food processor until it looks like snow. Then scoop about a half cup of the shaved ice on top of the beans.
  3. Add some banana and jackfruit slices, and top with another half cup of shaved ice. Add the macapuno strips and nata de coco.
  4. Process the other half of the ice cubes and add another half cup of shaved ice on top.
  5. Add the red and green palm seeds. Then add the final half cup of shaved ice to each glass, doming it slightly on top.
  6. Pour 1/2 cup evaporated milk on top of each parfait and top with a scoop of ube ice cream.
  7. Top with Cap'n Crunch cereal and enjoy immediately! Don't forget to halo-halo it with a long spoon!

Prep Time: 15 minutes
