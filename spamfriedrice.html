- - - Spam Fried Rice - - -

Ingredients Measurement
Cooked White Rice 2 cups
Beaten Eggs 2 pcs.
Sesame Oil 1 Tbsp.
Spam Meat 1 can
Mixed Vegetables 2 cups
Oil 1 Tbsp.
Stalk Green Onion 1 pc.
Procedure:
  1. Combine leftover rice, egg and sesame oil in a large bowl. Mix thoroughly with hand and set aside.
  2. Bring a medium pot of water to boil, add the frozen mixed vegetables into the pot, cook 1-2 minutes, drain and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a pan till shimmering over medium heat, add the spam cubes, stir-fry 3-4 minutes or until light golden brown. Transfer the spam cube to a small bowl and set aside, leave the oil in the pan.
  4. Carefully add the rice (mixed with egg and sesame oil), stir-fry 7-8 minutes or until the rice is no longer sticks together, became dry and fluffy (Means the egg has been cooked).
  5. Turn to high heat, add the pre-cooked mixed vegetables, stir-fry for a minute.
  6. Stir in the lightly browned spam cubes, stir-fry for another 1-2 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Transfer to a large plate, sprinkle with chopped green onion to serve.
  9. Drizzled with Sriracha, or add a fried egg on top if desired.

Prep Time: 30 minutes
